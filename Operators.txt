Operator is a keyword in python that represent an action or computation on one or more operands.

Types:
1. Arithimetic
    a. + : Addition
    b. - : Subraction
    c. * : Multiplication
    d. / : Division
    e. % : Modulus - Remainder
    f. **: Exponentiation
    g. //: Floor Division (Integer value, or quotient)

2. Assignment
    a. = (Simple Assignment)
    b. += (Add and Assign)
    c. -= (Subract and Assign)
    d. *=
    e. /=
    f. %=
    g. **=
    h. //=

3. Comparision (Output will be a boolean value)
    a. == 
    b. !=
    c. >
    d. <
    e. >=
    f. <=

4. Logical 
    a. and ((x >5) and (y<10))
    b. or  ((x >5) or (y<10))
    c. not (not (x <5))
    
5. Bitwise / Explain at the end of session 
    a. &
    b. |
    c. XOR(^)
    d. NOT (~)
    e. Left Shift (<<)
    f. Right Shift (>>)
    
6. Membership 
    a. in
    b. not in

7. Identity
    a. is
    b. is not
    c. Difference between == and is***
    
8. Special
9.ternary 
   true_value if condition else false_value
